I research and design tools to help make better public policy. I use a
complex adaptive system lens across a number of areas ranging from
inequality, transport, energy, disaster management and urban planning
to social protection.

I am the Co-Founder of Fields of View (https://www.fieldsofview.in/),
non-profit research group in Bangalore. In 2018, I was elected as an
Ashoka Fellow (https://bit.ly/bmpashoka) for my work in designing
tools and methods to allow different stakeholders to collaboratively
create public policy. I teach courses based on request, in the areas
of Tools for Participation in Public Policy, Games as Research Tools,
and Data in Public Policy.

I send out Poems by Post
(https://www.bharathpalavalli.com/poemsbypost.html), collect fountain
pens and occasionally sketch (which I share on PixelFed
(https://pixelfed.sdf.org/bmp) & Instagram
(https://pixelfed.sdf.org/bmp)), read SFF, try to speak Esperanto and,
am awaiting my amateur radio license.
