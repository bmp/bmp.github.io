Bharath M. Palavalli

I research and design tools to help make better public policy. I use the complex adaptive system lens in my work across a number of areas ranging from inequality, transport, energy, disaster management and urban planning to social protection.

I am the Co-Founder of Fields of View, a non-profit research group in Bangalore. In 2018, I was elected as an Ashoka Fellow for my work in designing tools and methods to allow different stakeholders to collaborate on creating relevant public policy. I teach courses based on request, in the areas of Tools for Participation in Public Policy, Games as Research Tools, and Data in Public Policy.
