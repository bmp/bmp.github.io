Bharath M. Palavalli

I research and design tools to help make better public policy. I use a complex adaptive system lens across a number of areas ranging from inequality, transport, energy, disaster management and urban planning to social protection.

I am the Co-Founder of Fields of View, a non-profit research group in Bangalore. In 2018, I was elected as an Ashoka Fellow for my work in designing tools and methods to allow different stakeholders to collaboratively create public policy. I teach courses based on request, in the areas of Tools for Participation in Public Policy, Games as Research Tools, and Data in Public Policy.

I send out Poems by Post, collect fountain pens and occasionally sketch (which I share on PixelFed & Instagram), read SFF, try to speak Esperanto, and am awaiting my amateur radio license. My validated keys are available on KeyOxide.
